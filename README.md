Netflix Tableau Dashboard

Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard developed to analyze Netflix content and uncover insights related to content distribution, ratings, release trends, and country-wise performance.

The dashboard enables users to explore Netflix’s catalog through dynamic visualizations and filters, helping identify patterns and trends within the dataset.

⸻

Business Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze Netflix’s content library and answer key business questions such as:

* Which countries contribute the most content?
* How has Netflix content grown over time?
* What are the most common content ratings?
* What is the distribution between Movies and TV Shows?
* Which release years contain the highest volume of content?

Key Insights

* Movies account for a larger share of Netflix content than TV Shows.
* The United States contributes the highest volume of content.
* Content growth accelerated significantly after 2015.
* TV-MA is among the most frequently occurring ratings.
* Netflix content is distributed across a wide range of countries and genres.
